Leading Edge (LE)

This is the combination of DFT plus Length.

LE can be neutral by moving the fin back by the same amount of increased length (and vice-versa).

Binding to Leading Edge (B-LE)

This is the distance between the leading edge of the fin and the point at which you measure your binding.

This can affect your turning radius.
